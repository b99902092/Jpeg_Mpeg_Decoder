MpegParser by Po-Kang Hsiao, CMLAB

This Program can dump the syntax, motion and VLC information during the 
decoding process. You can specify the frame number that you want to debug.

Usage:
   mpegparser [input file] [frame number]

Please contact the TA if you have any problem in usage, or any suggestion
,such as display style, that will facilitate the debugging process.
